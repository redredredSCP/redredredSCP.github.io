"untitled 05-01-2022"

Oh--She looks at me with terror
Counting seven in the night
You can turn all of the lights out
If you're scared of the respite
He never minded anyway--
He never minded cause it's easier
To just forget her name.
Oh--You're too good to follow orders
But that's where rests all your talent
She's the love of all her teachers
But her husband, she never found it
And when I counted nine
She still burned only to die
'Cause she knew how bad it would go
For the rest of her corrupt nothing life.

"poem for darby crash 04-28-2022"

And you heard Jesus speak to you
From deep inside your head
He said you could never follow through
On pulling out that thread
And you're in love with Bowie
Cause he was nothing then
And you love how he was thinner
Than other peoples' friends
And you were sad for your parents
Because they had to handle you
And you heard the way they wondered
Ways to dismantle you
And you never found nobody
As graceful or forlorn
Who prayed to God their mother died
The day before they were born
And I will never understand
The method you tried to find
But I know it's thankless
To be the one that's left behind
And I know it's worthless
To ask why you wanted to die
'Cause if I knew you better I'd know
You already made up your mind.

"untitled 05-08-2022"

Hello sir, could you teach me how to be a real punk?
I'm good at reading guides and I can pay a lump sum
I like Tori Amos and don't really fit in
With the girls at my school 'cause they like gentlemen
My favorite year for music was 1979
My favorite label's SST or maybe EMI
And Fugazi's okay, but he's a little too loud
Wish I could give him something so he could chill out
And I like all the fashion: all the pants and armbands
I like how all of the bands can somehow understand
I like watching the people who cry at the swastikas
I like how easy it is to see when they're not one of us
And when I see them at shows, I go and spit in their eyes
Cause he took a job that really oughta be mine
I can work all day and I can work all night
But I still got no money or any songs to write
And I try really hard but I still always lose
Wish it was Germany here; wish I could get some black boots
I'd be willing to starve or sleep for days on the ground
If for once I could stomp those other losers down
So I know this is weird for just an on-the-street meeting
But Mr. Rollins, could--Wait, why are you leaving?

"untitled 05-02-2022 1&2"

I worry.
I worry about many things:
Unrealistic things, things
That only women worry in
The throes of their dreams
But still I worry.
I worry about you the
Way you see me.
Though I realize, clawing at
That cliff above me
That what you were seeing
Was nothing.
Fuck me, I'm such a hyperreal thing
I exist only in your thoughts
When they catch your mind
Like lightning--
When they stare through your sheets
Keeping you up nightly
When they run you out of town,
A mob of nothing chasing nothing.
But I wouldn't exist without them
And so each night I worry thusly.
/
There is no purpose to worry
Broken machines break down daily
Their arms and legs smashing oceans
Beneath their immense, bloated weight
And you never say sorry
Though you claw to escape daily
The pillow softness of blinding clouds
Your head so full of soft sounds
You don't mind the endless wait
And when you descend it's only
To call open the chorus empty
And spill out pathetic nothings
To a crowd of nobody
And you smile and think of it
As something worth celebrating
But cool doesn't stop for
Anything or Anyone
It marches forward for
Another machine to burn down.
